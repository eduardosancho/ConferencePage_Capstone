Agricultural Technology Global Summit 2022

The Global Summit brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Agricultural Technology affiliate network in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals.

So, if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, Wikipedians, Open Knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments, and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together.

Please contact us per Email for any further questions about AT Global Summit 2022! contact@agriculturaltechnology.com
